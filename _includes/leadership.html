One of three sales executives (total of 21) to sell over $2,000,000 (6.6% of overall sales). Furthermore, he developed a client base in a broad geography throughout the western hemisphere & the pacific. The vast majority of said geography, the integrator had never had market share in.

He’d started in the industry at an optics factory (1993 - 1995).
